Project Motivation

The motivation behind this project is to bridge the communication gap in customer service for Kinyarwanda speakers. By developing an AI assistant that understands and responds in Kinyarwanda, we aim to provide better, more personalized customer care.

Challenges

Some of the challenges faced in this project include:

  • Limited resources for Kinyarwanda NLP development.
  • Ensuring the accuracy and naturalness of the AI's responses.
  • Integrating the AI model into existing customer care systems.
